2.Problem of moist piece of cloth
A moist piece of cloth in the open air loses its moisture at a rate proportional to the moisture content.If a sheet hung in the wind loses half its moisture during the first hour,when will it lose 99\% of moisture,weather conditions remaining unchanged?
To solve this problem found in our everyday life,let m0 be the initial moisture content and m be the value of m 2after t hours.
These facts can be put in the language of Calculus as
